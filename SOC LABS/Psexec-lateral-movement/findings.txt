Findings

The investigation identified multiple indicators consistent with PsExec-mediated lateral movement within the environment.

Observed activity included:
- Sequential SYN scanning activity
- SMB communications between internal hosts
- Access requests to IPC$ and ADMIN$ administrative shares
- SMB Write Requests involving PSEXESVC.EXE
- DCERPC and SVCCTL traffic associated with remote service creation

The evidence strongly supports remote administrative execution and lateral movement activity originating from host 10.0.0.130 toward host 10.0.0.133.
